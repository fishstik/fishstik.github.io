Rules

    Gameplay

    G1. Be cool. Try to call fouls on yourself and play the game fairly.

    G2. Do not make contact while the Frisbee is in the air. Incidental contact is OK if the player is going for the Frisbee.

    G3. You may not contact the thrower while marking him. Similarly, he may not move you aside in order to throw the Frisbee. The marker may knock the Frisbee out of the throwers hands, but it does not result in a turnover, and the play is redone.

    G4. The thrower has 10 seconds to throw the Frisbee before it is a turnover.

    G5. Be sportsmanlike. Unfriendly taunting or unsportsmanlike contact may result in suspensions or expulsion.

    G6. No steps may be taken with the Frisbee. Players can only be allowed to let their momentum stop running.


    League

    L1. The schedule will run until June 18. Games may be rescheduled for reasons such as weather, but will not be rescheduled due to lack of players.

    L2. Playoffs will consist of the best two teams from each division, plus the next best two records from either division. In the result of a tie, the team which won the contest in the regular season will make playoffs. If the two teams split 1-1, then the team which scored the most amount of points throughout the season will advance to the playoffs.

    L3. The commissioner has the right to suspend and expel players who do not abide to the given rules.

    L4. Teams MAY NOT recruit players from other teams or non-league players to play for their team. Doing so will result in a forfeit or deduction of points.

    L5. All trades must be verified by the commissioner before they can join the team. Games played with unverified players will result in forfeit. The trade deadline was June 11. No more trades are allowed.

    L6. If a team arrives at a game with less than 6 teammates, the opposing team may force a forfeit and win the game.


    Playoffs

    P1. If opposing players catch a frisbee at the same time and one does not manage to grab it by the time they land, offense receives the frisbee.

    P2. No steps with the frisbee! If you see someone taking unreasonably many steps after catching, call it out. How strictly this rule will be enforced depends on if someone calls it out. Only then will a turnover take place.

    P3. Spectators cannot harass players if it is obviously bothering them. Harassing spectators will be dealt with by other spectators.

    P4. Games will be played to whatever both captains agree on, win by 2.
